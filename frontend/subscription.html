Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings with one click.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major debit and credit cards through our secure Paystack integration.

Will I lose access to my invoices if I cancel?

No, you'll retain access to all your existing invoices and data. You just won't be able to create new premium invoices.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use Paystack for payment processing. Your card details are never stored on our servers and all transactions are encrypted.

When will Gold and Opal be available?

Payla Gold is launching in Q1 2026, while Payla Opal remains invite-only for elite brands and agencies.
